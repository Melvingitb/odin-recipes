Pad Krapow

Description

This recipe shows you how to make a popular Thai dish, Pad Krapow. You can use any sort of meat or tofu youd like, but this recipe uses chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, coarsely chopped
  • 2 cups hot cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup sliced shallots
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons minced Thai chiles

Steps

  1. Whisk chicken broth, oyster sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, white sugar, and brown sugar in a bowl.
  2. Stir fry chicken over high heat with oil for 2-3 minutes until it loses its raw color.
  3. Stir in shallots, garlic, and sliced chiles. Cook for 2-3 more minutes.
  4. Add a tablespoon of sauce mixture to the skillet and cook for about 1 minute.
  5. Pour in rest of sauce and cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  6. Stir in basil for about 20 seconds.
  7. Serve hot with rice and enjoy!
