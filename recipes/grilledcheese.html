Grilled Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for a classic grilled cheese.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the toast with 1/2 Tbsp butter.
  2. Heat skillet in low-medium heat and add bread.
  3. Add cheese to one of the toast, stack the other toast on top, then flip.
  4. cook until bread is golden brown and cheese is melted.
  5. Cut sandwich diagonally then serve!
