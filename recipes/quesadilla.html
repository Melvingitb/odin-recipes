Quesadilla

Description

This is a recipe for a simple quesadilla.

Ingredients

  • 1 tortilla
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup cooked black beans
  • 1 tablespoon chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon chopped pickled jalapeño
  • 1 teaspoon melted butter

Steps

  1. Heat skillet over medium heat and heat tortilla for 30 seconds, flipping halfway.
  2. Sprinkle half of cheese onto half of the tortilla.
  3. Cover cheese with remaining fillings: beans, bell peppers, onion, jalapeño.
  4. Sprinkle remaining cheese over fillings then fold tortilla over. Brush the top of tortilla with a light coat of butter, then flip.
  5. Cook quesadilla for about 1-2 minutes, or until tortilla is crispy and golden. Brush top with butter, then flip once again and cook.
  6. Remove quesadilla from heat and let cool for about a minute. Cut it into the desired amount of pieces and serve.
